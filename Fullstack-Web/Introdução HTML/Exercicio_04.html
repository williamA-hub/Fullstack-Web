The Best Movies According to William

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Blue Book Project

The Project Blue Book series captures my heart with its engaging blend of US Air Force UFO investigations and delving into the mysteriesof the unknown, combining elements of science fiction and drama with captivating characters.

The Hobbit

The Hobbit film trilogy captivates me with its enchanting journey through Middle-earth, rich character development, and the epic quest to reclaim the Lonely Mountain from the fearsome dragon Smaug.

Fullmetal Alchemist

Fullmetal Alchemist anime holds a special place in my heart due to its intricate world-building, compelling characters, and the alchemical brothers' emotive journey to restore their bodies while uncovering dark truths in a society rife with corruption.
